What is Expresive Art

Expressive art is a form of art that is used in a emotional aspect. It allows us to be intuitive with various of media. Expressive art allows us to go deep into our realms to discover our emotions and express them in drama, visual art, music, writing, and even movement.

I love expressive art because I can show my create styles in any way shape or form in a emotional way. I also admire the fact that expressive art doesn't only come in drawing.This is the best way for me to express my emotional state.
